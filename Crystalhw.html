The Game of Champions!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four championship trophies below. By clicking on a trophy you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each trophy is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each trophy.
